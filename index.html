SmartBox is a secure metal locker system with QR-code access for storing or collecting items. It offers 24/7 contactless access, enhanced security, and convenient keyless entry. Items are placed in a locker, a unique QR code is generated and sent to the recipient, and scanning it instantly unlocks the box for retrieval.
