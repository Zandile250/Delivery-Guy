Next Generation Intelligent Storage

SmartBox is a secure metal locker system with QR-code access for storing or collecting items. It offers 24/7 contactless access, enhanced security, and convenient keyless entry.

Our Journey

January 2022

Project kickoff and initial planning completed
This initiative is all about reimagining traditional practices to better serve our customers—making parcel handling more convenient, secure, and aligned with their best interests. We’re excited to move forward with a fresh approach that puts customer experience at the heart of everything we do.

March 2023

First prototype developed and internal testing conducted.
It’s a big step forward, and we’re excited about what’s taking shape. The early version has already given us valuable feedback, helping us fine-tune the experience and make sure it truly meets the needs of our customers. This isn’t just about tech—it’s about making things simpler, safer, and more intuitive for the people who rely on us. We’re now gearing up for the next phase, where we’ll keep refining and improving based on real-world insights.

June 2024

Beta version released to selected users for feedback.
It’s a big moment for us—seeing something we’ve worked so hard on finally land in real hands. This phase is all about listening closely. We want to know what feels good, what’s confusing, and where we can do better. Every bit of feedback helps us shape something that’s not just functional, but genuinely useful and easy to love. We’re incredibly grateful to those testing it and helping us make it better, one insight at a time.

September 2025

Final release launched with full features.
It’s been a journey—from early ideas and prototypes to testing and refining—and now we’re here. Seeing it come together is incredibly rewarding. Every detail has been shaped by real feedback, thoughtful design, and a shared commitment to making things better for our customers. This isn’t just a product release—it’s a moment to celebrate how far we’ve come and look ahead to the impact we’re about to make.
