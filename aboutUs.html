About Us

SmartBox was founded with a simple mission: to make storage smarter, safer, and more convenient. From our first prototype to our award-winning rollout, we’ve reimagined how people interact with lockers—turning a traditional concept into a modern, tech-powered solution.

We started in 2022 with a vision to eliminate queues, reduce contact, and empower users with 24/7 access. Today, SmartBox is trusted by individuals, students, businesses, and institutions across South Africa.

Our Services

SmartBox is a secure, self-service locker system powered by QR-code access. Whether it’s picking up an online order, collecting a package, or storing personal items, SmartBox makes the process simple and contactless.

We go beyond parcel storage—offering smart lockers for food, work equipment, and personal belongings. With 24/7 access and app-based management, SmartBox is built for flexibility and peace of mind.

Who Can Use SmartBox?

  • 👤 Individuals – Store seasonal items, furniture, or personal belongings
  • 🎓 Students – Keep items safe between semesters or during holidays
  • 🏪 Small Business Owners – Secure inventory, tools, or documents
  • 🚚 People Moving or Renovating – Temporary storage for household items
  • 🔐 Anyone Who Values Security & Convenience – Enjoy QR-code access and app control
