SmartBox is a secure, self-service locker system that works with QR-code access. Whether it’s picking up an online order, collecting a package, or storing personal items for a short time, SmartBox makes the process simple and contactless. Customers receive a unique QR code, scan it at the box, and instantly access their items — no queues, no staff needed, and available 24/7.
