Our Services

SmartBox is a secure, self-service locker system that works with QR-code access. Whether it’s picking up an online order, collecting a package, or storing personal items for a short time, SmartBox makes the process simple and contactless. Customers receive a unique QR code, scan it at the box, and instantly access their items — no queues, no staff needed, and available 24/7.

Not only do we offer secure storage, but we go beyond to provide smart lockers for all kinds of needs — from picking up parcels and online orders to storing personal items, food, or work equipment. Each locker is QR-code powered, making access quick, contactless, and available 24/7. Whether it’s a package, a meal, or something valuable, SmartBox keeps it safe until you’re ready to collect.

Who Can Use SmartBox

  • Individuals: People who need extra space at home for seasonal items, furniture, or personal belongings.
  • Students: For storing items between semesters or during holidays.
  • Small Business Owners: To store inventory, equipment, or documents.
  • People Moving or Renovating: Temporary storage of household items.
  • Anyone Who Values Security and Convenience: SmartBox uses QR-code access and app-based management for safe and easy access.
